Where One Mag is today

Reading time 2 minutes

Business has always been the biggest driver of economics from the beginning of time. The chain of human life depends on the power of money and what drives the economy. A keen interest drove me into a research to find out what really drives the economy and there were many facts depending on my environment. It changes rapidly and with the times. Being an open system, it relies on the changes of the environment and responds accordingly.

This magazine illustrates the most common methods of conducting underrated business practices that could underline the problems in making an efficient working atmosphere. Written with an open mind to how the industry works, this should open discussion onto what is accurate or not, due to the fact that a good number of people are born into the business environment and are already determined to be the customer. Breaking free from the chain to be the suppler or retailer could be a challenge that needs efficient analysis.

In the end, we may conclude that business is in our nature. The urge to exchange goods to improve each other's lives has been there. Though with the complex nature of the world these days, good practice has never been harder to find or achieve. Quality products and services are even harder to come by. A time where discipline and good ethics has been long forgotten. It seems that the folks that uphold the up most quality tend to keep the mystery of success to themselves while presenting their earnings at once. The truth being we could all be a better part of ourselves, but with the higher demand of self care at cost. It seems that it would be more logical for one to cater for their needs first before others. This is where One Magazine tries to change th minds of people who need empathy in what they do to change the world for the betterment of humanity.
