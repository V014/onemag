Where One Mag is today

Reading time 2 minutes

People spend a good portion of their time being entertained, through out the lifespan of human civilization, there has been countless forms of entertainment. From dance and art, all the way to music. But for each form, there is evolution of technique to which the entertainment takes form. This issue shows how that entertainment has shifted between culture and time.

To reduce the strain on production, music and fashion has been merged into one issue. Sometimes people get entertained by the clothes we wear. As part of this I dedicated sections depicting this. Depending on the reception, fashion might return but the chances are my sense of fashion is subjective to prospective and body build. This tells us that what I might see cool, the next person will not see it the same.

Artists has been mentioned but mainly from the year 2023, so much has changed and people have evolved as well. The next issue will capture the milestones artists have taken during 2025 and where music is going from there. I have also dedicated a section on music production showing young artists what it's like to create music as a team. Most of the time people like to work alone and truely it could be left to be desired. Abstract music shows how music is a blend of culture and it should not die out because someone did not want to share their prospective.
